About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer and UI/UX Designer completing my Web Development and Design diploma at NAIT. My background is in architecture, and I've carried that same eye for structure and detail into everything I build on the web. I think of it as internet architecture.

I'm multilingual (English, Russian, Hebrew) and bring a strong design sensibility to every project. Whether I'm building from scratch in HTML/CSS/JS, spinning up a React app, configuring a WordPress theme, or designing wireframes in Figma, I focus on clean code, intuitive UI, and work that actually looks good.

I've built real websites for real clients: a gaming community hub, a professional painter's business site, and a personal creative space for my wife, all while running multiple course projects simultaneously. I'm comfortable across the full stack and I know how to ship.

After graduating, I'll be joining Netflix as a Full-Stack Developer. I'm always looking to keep building things in the meantime.
