About Me

My background is in architecture, and I carry that same structural thinking and eye for detail into every interface I build. I think of it as internet architecture. That precision is the core of my brand: coiled, deliberate, and built to last.

I work across the full stack: HTML/CSS/JS, React, PHP, MySQL, WordPress, Figma. I bring a strong design sensibility to everything I touch, whether that's wireframing in Figma, building out a custom WordPress theme, or coding a site from scratch. I've shipped real projects for real clients and I know how to manage timelines, handle feedback cycles, and get things done.

I'm also multilingual, fluent in English and Russian and conversational in Hebrew, and I've spent several years in leadership and client-facing roles before pivoting fully into web. That background shows up in how I communicate, collaborate, and take ownership of a project.
